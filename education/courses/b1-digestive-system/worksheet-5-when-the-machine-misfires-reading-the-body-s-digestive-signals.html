Coaching boundary
A symptom is a signal, not a diagnosis. This worksheet builds the literacy to read signals backwards to their mechanisms, not a license to diagnose or treat. Read everyday signals as signals, but the moment an alarm feature appears (blood, unexplained weight loss, anemia, trouble swallowing, persistent vomiting, nighttime symptoms, a sustained change in bowel habit, or new symptoms after 50), stop theorizing and refer promptly to a qualified clinician.
