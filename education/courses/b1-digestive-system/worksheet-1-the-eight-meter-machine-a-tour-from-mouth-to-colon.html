Where education ends
This worksheet builds digestive literacy, not a license to diagnose or treat, in yourself or anyone else. Explain how the system works, and treat anything that looks clinical (swallowing difficulty, persistent pain, blood, unexplained weight loss, or other alarm signs) as warranting evaluation by a qualified clinician. Work within your role: a coach refers, a clinician works within their license, and a self-learner does not self-diagnose.
