Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026 — Please review with a legal professional before publishing.

What information I collect

When you use the contact form or purchase a service, I may collect your name, email address, phone number, and any information you voluntarily provide. Corrective bodywork and training clients may provide health-related information as part of onboarding.

How I use your information

Information collected is used solely to respond to inquiries, deliver purchased services, and communicate with active clients. I do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Use of results for advertising

I reserve the right to use client results, progress photos, and testimonials for the continued promotion and advertising of my services. If you prefer that your face, full name, or social media handles not be included in any advertising, that option is completely available and will be fully respected. Simply let me know and your discretion will be honored without question.

Payment information

Payment processing is handled by a third-party provider. I do not store your payment details. Please refer to your payment processor's privacy policy for details on how that data is handled.

Communication

Active clients may communicate via WhatsApp as part of the coaching service. By providing your contact information and engaging in coaching, you consent to communication through that channel.

Your rights

You may request that your personal data be deleted at any time by contacting me directly. Requests will be honored within a reasonable timeframe.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, reach out via the contact page.
