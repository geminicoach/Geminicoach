What you'll learn

What's Inside.

Built for anyone who wants to understand nutrition without the noise — from the basics of macros all the way through metabolism and supplementation.

01
Macronutrients

Protein, carbohydrates, and fats explained — what each does, how much you need, and why getting the balance right changes everything.

02
Micronutrients

Vitamins and minerals most people ignore — their roles in energy, recovery, hormones, and long-term health.

03
Metabolism Explained

How your body actually burns calories — BMR, TDEE, TEF, NEAT, and EAT broken down in plain language so you can actually use the information.

04
Energy Systems

How your body produces and uses energy — and what that means for fat loss, performance, and how you should be eating around training.

05
Supplementation Basics

A no-nonsense breakdown of common supplements — what the research actually supports, what's overhyped, and where to start.

06
Physiology Fundamentals

A moderate deep dive into the body systems behind nutrition — digestion, hormones, and how your physiology responds to what you eat.

The Author

Parker JamesGruenhagen.

Parker is a nutrition coach, physiotherapist, and NPC competitor with nearly a decade of experience working with clients across all levels of fitness. He has spent years translating complex nutrition and physiology concepts into language that actually makes sense — and this book is the result of that work. Whether you're starting from scratch or filling in gaps in your understanding, The Nutrition Blueprint gives you the foundation to make informed decisions about how you eat and train.
